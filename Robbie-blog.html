The Machine has Gone Away!

Return home

Mrs. Weston:


When we first got Robbie, Mr. Weston promised me Gloria wouldn't get any connection to him. Here I am, 2 years later, having seen my daughter 2 times all day, ready to get rid of Robbie. He has been raising my daughter! I've talked to George plenty of times about this, but he doesn't understand. He tells me all my fears are irrational. Whenever I talk to him, George dismisses my worries and tells me Robbie is helping Gloria. I say, "Robbie is a robot. Metal connected to electricity and he has no soul. He can't be raising our daughter. What if Gloria gets hurt?"

  "First off, Robbie cannot hurt any person, including Gloria. That is the first and most important rule for all robots. Robbie was made for only one purpose, to be a companion for a child like Gloria. Just because he doesn't have a soul, he can still be a good friend for Gloria. Gloria loves him, isn't that good enough reason to keep him, Gloria being happy?" and we stopped talking after that. George wouldn't talk about it anymore, until last night. Finally, George listened to me and we can take the hunk of metal to the factory! I cannot describe how happy I am. I no longer need to worry about Robbie or Gloria, and can live the rest of my life knowing I didn't let a machine hurt my daughter.
